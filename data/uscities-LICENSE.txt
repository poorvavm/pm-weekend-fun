data/uscities.csv is derived from the SimpleMaps United States Cities Database
(Basic version 1.79), available at https://simplemaps.com/data/us-cities.

Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).
See: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

The columns retained in this repo are: city, state_id, state_name, lat, lng.
The original dataset includes additional fields (population, county, zips, etc.)
that have been dropped to reduce file size.
